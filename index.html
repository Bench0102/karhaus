WHO WE ARE

Karhaus Detailing is Atlanta's trusted name in premium auto detailing. We specialize in ceramic coatings, paint correction, and paint protection for luxury and exotic vehicles. Our team combines cutting-edge technology with time-honored techniques to deliver exceptional results.

With an unwavering commitment to excellence, every vehicle we service turns heads on the road. We treat every car with the same attention to detail, whether it's a daily driver or a prized collector's item.
